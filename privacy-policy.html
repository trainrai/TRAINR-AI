Privacy Policy for TRAINR AI

Last updated: September 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

TRAINR AI is a fitness application that helps you track your workouts and fitness goals. We collect the following information:

1.1 Google Sign-In Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (as provided by Google)
  • Your Google profile picture (optional)

1.2 App Usage Data

We may collect:

  • Workout data you input into the app
  • Fitness goals and progress
  • App usage statistics to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our fitness tracking services
  • Personalize your workout experience
  • Track your fitness progress
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure the security and functionality of our app

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@trainrai.com

This privacy policy is effective as of September 28, 2025.
